Frequently asked questions

Your plumbing questions — answered honestly

Do you offer 24/7 emergency plumbing service?

Yes. For urgent issues like major leaks, backups, and no-water situations, request emergency support and we will prioritize the appointment.

What counts as a plumbing emergency?

A plumbing emergency is any issue that can cause water damage, sanitation problems, or a safety risk if left alone.

Can you fix a leaking pipe?

Yes. We diagnose the leak source, explain the repair, and help prevent related damage where possible.

Do you service water heaters?

Yes. We service traditional and tankless water heaters, including repairs, replacements, and troubleshooting.

Do you install fixtures?

Yes. Faucets, toilets, garbage disposals, tubs, showers, and other fixtures can be installed or replaced.
