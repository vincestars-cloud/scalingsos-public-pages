SMS Message Templates

These save to the Prospector Config SSOT and are read by n8n. Msg 2 branches by whether the business already has a website and rotates A/B/C per branch.

Supported tokens with examples
{name}, {business}, {business_name} -> Cutler Bay Plumbing (humanized from the business name; LLC/Inc/etc removed)
{raw_business_name} -> CUTLER BAY PLUMBING LLC
{trade} / {search_term} -> Plumbing from the sheet search_term
{city} -> Cutler Bay from the sheet city
{noun} -> jobs, customers, clients, or projects based on industry
{link} / {demo_url} -> https://demo.scalingsos.com/cutler-bay-plumbing/
{website} / {website_url} -> their current website, if captured
{maps_url} -> their Google Maps profile URL
{phone} -> the contact phone number
