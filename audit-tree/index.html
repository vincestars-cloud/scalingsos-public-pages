Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions From All Our Clients

How much does tree removal cost in Austin?
Full removal, trimming and pruning, stump grinding, storm cleanup, brush hauling and crane-assisted removal are each priced after a free on-site estimate, based on tree size, location and access.
Do you provide emergency tree services?
Yes — we offer 24/7 emergency response for storm damage and hazardous trees. Call any time and we'll dispatch a crew as fast as conditions allow.
How often should trees be trimmed or pruned?
Most trees benefit from pruning every 3–5 years; young and fast-growing species may need it more often. We'll assess yours during the estimate.
Do you clean up after the job is done?
Always. We haul away all debris, rake the area and leave your property cleaner than we found it — cleanup is included in your quote.
What tree services do you offer?
Full tree removal, trimming and pruning, stump grinding, storm cleanup, brush hauling and crane-assisted removal — including the larger, more complex jobs other crews turn down.
