STRAIGHT ANSWERS

Questions homeowners actually ask

Will my insurance actually cover this?

Most sudden, accidental water damage is covered by standard homeowner policies. Gradual leaks and some mold cases vary. We document everything to industry standards and communicate clearly with your adjuster.

How fast can you get here?

For active emergencies we dispatch 24/7 and are typically on-site within about an hour across our core metro service area.

Is the mold in my home dangerous?

It depends on the type, amount, and who lives in the home. We can test, contain the area, and give you clear next steps based on evidence instead of fear.

What does it cost?

It depends on the source, size, and how long it has been there. When insurance applies, your out-of-pocket is often your deductible. We provide a clear scope before work begins.

Do I need to leave my home during the work?

Usually not. For most jobs we contain the affected area so the rest of your home stays livable. Larger or sensitive situations are discussed upfront.

Do not wait for water to turn into mold.

Every hour water sits, the damage and cost can climb. Get a free assessment now and we’ll tell you exactly where you stand.
